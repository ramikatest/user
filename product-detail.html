Kibena Tea Company

In Summary

Kibena Tea Company is a well-known tea producer in Njombe town, located in the southern highlands of Tanzania. The company has been operating for several decades and has built a strong reputation for producing high-quality tea products. Njombe town, situated in the lush green hills of the Southern Highlands, provides the perfect climate and soil conditions for growing tea, which has contributed to the success of Kibena Tea Company.
